/ 05 — How We Build

THE PROCESS

01
Design & CAD
Full cutting diagrams and 3D renders before a single panel is cut. Every millimetre planned.
02
CNC Cutting
Precision panel saw with ±0.1mm tolerance. Supermatt lacquer surfaces protected throughout.
03
Edge Banding
Colour-matched PVC/ABS banding laser-glued. Flat face panel edges sealed perfectly same-colour.
04
Drilling
CNC drilling for hinges, dowels, EASYTOUCH push mechanisms and LINE N recessed tracks.
05
Assembly
Quality-inspected at every stage. Supermatt surfaces protected until final installation.
06
Installation
Our team delivers, fits, and levels every unit in Addis Ababa. First measurement to final fit.

Klever Addis Trading P.L.C. brings European-grade supermatt kitchen systems to Ethiopia. Founded 2017 EC in Addis Ababa.

We manufacture and install flat-face handleless fronts — the same supermatt lacquer systems from Nobilia Germany — using CNC routers, edgebanders, and push-to-open hardware.

Every project fully custom: your space, your finish, your layout. Designed, built, and installed by our own team.
