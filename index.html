/ 05 — Company

About Us

Klever Addis Trading P.L.C.

We are a modern furniture manufacturer based in Addis Ababa, established in 2017 EC. We specialize in modular kitchens, wardrobes, bathroom vanities and custom MDF/wood projects.

Our factory uses CNC routers, edgebanders, nesting and assembly lines to deliver high quality products meeting international standards.

From first measurement to final installation — every detail handled with precision and care for your home or office.
